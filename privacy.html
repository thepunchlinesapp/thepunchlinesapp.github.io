Privacy Policy - Punchlines

Introduction

Welcome to the privacy policy of the Punchlines application. We place great importance on the protection of your personal data.

Data Collected

Our application only collects information that you provide during the initial onboarding:

  • Your content preferences
  • Your personalization choices
  • Your usage settings

No personally identifiable information is collected without your explicit consent.

Use of Data

All collected data is anonymized and used exclusively for:

  • Improving your user experience
  • Personalizing content according to your preferences
  • Analyzing overall app usage for improvement purposes

Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored on Supabase servers, with security protocols that comply with industry standards. Data is anonymized before any analytical processing.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, exchange, or transfer your anonymized information to third parties, except in the following cases:

  • When necessary to provide a service you have requested
  • To comply with legal obligations

In-App Purchases

If you make in-app purchases via RevenueCat, please note that we only have access to transaction data, not your payment information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request rectification or deletion of this data
  • Object to the processing of your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. The changes will take effect as soon as they are published in the application or on our website.

Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy or your personal data, please contact us at:

support@thepunchlines.app
